## 📞 **FAQ FOR DONORS (Share Widely)**

**Q: Is this secure? Will my blood data be private?**
A: Yes. Banking-grade security (AES-256 encryption), HIPAA-compliant, access logs audited.

**Q: What if I respond but can't donate that day?**
A: That's fine. You lose no privilege. System just notes "declined" and moves to next donor.

**Q: How fast do I need to respond?**
A: Ideally within 60 seconds. But even 5 minutes is helpful (vs 45+ min calling).

**Q: Will I be bothered too much?**
A: Only when there's a real emergency match for your blood type. ~2-3x per month on average.

**Q: Can I turn notifications off?**
A: Yes. You control your own availability in the app.

**Q: What if my blood type changed?**
A: Update in your profile. System uses latest info.

**Q: Do I get paid for donations?**
A: No. Donations are voluntary, as always. But you get immense personal satisfaction.
